## 🔧 Why This Fix Was Needed:

**The Problem:**
- Vercel's `vercel.json` has: `"outputDirectory": "website-v2"`
- This means Vercel ONLY serves files from the `website-v2` folder
- Your `data.json` was in the parent folder (`vision care/data.json`)
- So Vercel couldn't access it!

**The Solution:**
- Moved/copy data.json into `website-v2/data.json`
- Now Vercel can read and serve your admin data
- Your live website will load all the data you added in the admin panel
